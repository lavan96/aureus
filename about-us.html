About Us

Aureus Energy Holdings Pte. Ltd. is a Singapore-based special purpose vehicle (SPV) incorporated as investment holding company for the business venture in natural gas and liquefied natural gas (LNG) both in infrastructure and supply chain. AEH is wholly owned by Aureus LNG GmbH, incorporated out of Zug, Switzerland. Aureus LNG focus on developing midstream and downstream LNG infrastructure across Asia Pacific and South America. Aureus LNG taps into project development opportunities with strong domestic demand and rely on strong relationship with local project partner(s) for Final Investment Decision (FID) deliverables. These projects include onshore/offshore regasification and import terminal and LNG to Power projects which provides substantial baseload capacity undertakes.

As Aureus investment arm, our strategy for supply chain venture will be through Special Purpose Acquisition Company (SPAC) in United States of America. Through this strategy, we are in the midst of filing an IPO listing of Integrated Energy Transaction Acquisition Corporation (NYSE: ETA). Subject to SEC approval, we are raising between $150 - $300 million which later to be utilized for propose business combination with existing US gas aggregator/gas marketer. Post combination with valuation more than $1.5 billion, it will provide a new commercial model for gas buyers in Asia Pacific and South America ( and potentially some European customers). This new commercial model will be key differentiation with other major LNG suppliers with strong robust US domestic gas market as the hedger.

Mission Statement:

  • Focus on infrastructure and supply chain of energy transition fuel i.e Liquefied Natural gas (LNG), Blue/ Green Hydrogen, Ammonia and etc.
  • As an early developer for energy transition infrastructure projects either in upstream, midstream and downstream
  • Supplying the energy transition fuel into Asia Pacific as main market from most economical and abundance sources with view of providing at economical price and scale.

Vision Statement:

  • To be the preferred choice of energy transition provider both in infrastructure or supply chain activities throughout Asia Pacific and South America
