About Us

Aureus Energy Holdings Pte. Ltd. is incorporated as investment holding company for the business venture in natural gas, liquefied natural gas (LNG), hydrogen and other low to zero carbon emission fuel both in infrastructure and supply chain. The infrastructure business venture will be consolidated through ownership in LNG upstream projects with other potential downstream infrastructure projects in pipeline.

We also looking into establishing our presence in the supply chain on of natural gas or liquified natural gas (LNG) through investment in mid-upstream segment. Due to the abundance of the supply source, we believe we will able to provide a significant value through a cost effective commercial model. This consolidated commercial model is poised to bring more value to the customers as compared to the traditional conservative pricing model/index.

Aureus is developing a fully integrated international natural gas business through a three-pillared strategy:

  • developing an integrated international natural gas supply chain that provides seamless integration between natural gas supply from the United States to power generation facilities operating and being developed in the Asia-Pacific and South America regions;
  • developing local regasification projects and other downstream LNG infrastructure in the Asia Pacific andSouth America regions that service power generation developers and industrial natural gas consumers in those regions; and
  • originating projects that would expedite global fuel transition to natural gas by moving power generation and industrial consumers in the Asia Pacific and South America regions toward Aureus’ integrated natural gas supply chain,while preserving the commercial benefit these consumers have had prior to transitioning to natural gas.

Aureus seeks to work with one or more partners to provide optimal commercial and technical solutions in developing LNG value chain oriented projects, and Aureus is currently evaluating several possible early- stage LNG infrastructure projects under development in Indonesia, Malaysia,South Korea, and Brazil that are intended to meet fuel transition-related demand growth for natural gas in these markets. Aureus’ ultimate mission is to integrate LNG supply, infrastructure and logistics to enable power generation growth throughout the Asia-Pacific and South America regions by combining proven technology, know-how and experience that are fully compatible with the global LNG supply chain
